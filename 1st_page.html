What is a robot?

Robots are things that replicate work. They mainly replicate repeatable stuff as it is easier to program.

When was the first robot made?

The first robot was made in 1954 by George C. Devol who lived in Louisville, Kentucky. The robot is named Unimate and it automated metalworking and welding processes.

When was the word robot first invented?

The word robot was first made in 1920 and was used in K. Capek’s play R.U.R. ‘Rossum’s Universal Robots’. The word comes from Czech, as robota or ‘forced labor’.

When was the first myth about a robot?
